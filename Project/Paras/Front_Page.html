About

Eventex is a platform that provides information about various events happening in the university. It is a one-stop solution for all the students to know about the events and participate in them. The platform is designed to provide a seamless experience to the users and make the process of event participation easier.

Events

Experience the power of creativity and inspiration at our events. We bring together artists, thinkers, and innovators from across different fields to spark new ideas and share their stories.

Clubs
